Be the Cowboy

2018

Be the Cowboy should have won the Grammy for Album of the Year, and it's not even close. Mitski deals in heartbreak and loss, but she copes with pop synthesizers loud enough to cover up her angst. She makes sad songs you can dance to, and that's a difficult line to thread. She has a powerhouse voice that shakes and rambles with ease. Dex's only qualm with this album is the length: it should be at least 15 minutes longer, which might be one of the reasons Mitski lost out on the Grammy.
