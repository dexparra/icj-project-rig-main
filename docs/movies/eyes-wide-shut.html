Eyes Wide Shut

1999

Eyes Wide Shut is Dex's favorite Stanley Kubrick movie, which is saying a lot considering he's seen Kubrick's entire filmography. This one in particular is so captivating because of Nicole Kidman and Tom Cruises' performances, as well as the subject matter of infidelity in a marriage. Dex loves movies that deal with the inner workings of people's minds, and underground cults also pique his interest. He loves the grand set designs and the elaborate camera angles and movements.
